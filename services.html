Projects

Mentoring Program

We have established a mentoring program in order to support you in the areas of academia, research, career counseling, and anything else of benefit by pairing you up with an upperclassmen of similar interest.

Transition to New York

This program is specifically designed for helping students who will spend at least a semester in New York to make the most out of their experiences by connecting them with the resources and initiatives available in New York well in advance.

Technical Workshops

Through a series of lunchtime talks students get to learn from researchers and industry experts in the STEM fields.

Career Development Workshops

weSTEM organizes panels and workshops to facilitate you in finding and applying for your summer internships as well as postgraduate opportunities.

High School Outreach

weSTEM is organizing a one-day conference for high school girls in Abu Dhabi where they will have the chance of learning about career prospects with a STEM degree and the opportunity to work on hands-on projects in labs of the Science, Engineering and Interactive Media Departments of NYUAD.

The STEMinist Podcast

The STEMinist Podcast series works with NYUAD's Howler Radio to produce weekly shows covering different topics involving women in STEM fields, and STEM at NYUAD
